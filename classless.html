Images

Images are made block-level, rounded and given a drop shadow.

They also have a maximum width of 100% to stop them flowing over the page.

Blockquotes

A block quotation (also known as a long quotation or extract) is a quotation in a written document, that is set off from the main text as a paragraph, or block of text.

It is typically distinguished visually using indentation and a different typeface or smaller size quotation. It may or may not include a citation, usually placed at the bottom.

Buttons

Buttons are coloured in the primary colour and the secondary colour on hover, but this can be changed in the settings.

You can use classes to make buttons "small", "pill" shaped, "outline" only, or "clear".

You can also make other elements into a button by adding the class of "button"

a.button a.button.outline a.button.clear
